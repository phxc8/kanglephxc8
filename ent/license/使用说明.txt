使用方法
centos 6
执行运行升级命令，免费版将会升级到商业版
yum -y install wget;wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1265578519/kangle/master/ent/e.sh -O e.sh;sh e.sh /vhs/kangle

然后把授权文件license.txt复制到/vhs/kangle目录下，然后执行重启kangle命令
/vhs/kangle/bin/kangle -q
/vhs/kangle/bin/kangle


windows
32位：https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1265578519/kangle/master/ent/kangle-ent-3.4.8.msi

64位：https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1265578519/kangle/master/ent/kangle-ent-3.4.8-x64.msi
